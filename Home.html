Why Criminal Justice Matters to You

Imagine the adrenaline rush of a high-stakes arrest or the profound relief of a wrongful conviction overturned. Criminal justice isn't just about laws; it's about human stories, rights, and the delicate balance between freedom and security. Did you know that over 2 million people are incarcerated in the U.S. alone, and that false confessions contribute to about 25% of wrongful convictions? These facts remind us of the emotional stakes involved.

Fun Fact: The famous "Miranda warning" – "You have the right to remain silent..." – is recited in police stations across the country every day, protecting millions from self-incrimination. Another random tidbit: The Fourth Amendment, guarding against unreasonable searches, was inspired by British writs of assistance that colonists despised during the Revolutionary era.
